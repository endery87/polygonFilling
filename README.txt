 After running the program, user selects corners of the polygon he wants to draw by clicking 
left button of the mouse. When he decides to fill the polygon which consists of the corners he 
selected, he should press right button of the mouse. The user may clean the polygons and corners 
he drew by clicking the 'n' button on the keyboard.

We can say that ;

	- Left button of the mouse is used for corner selection.
	- Right button of the mouse is used for polygon filling. 
	- 'n' button of the keyboard is used for cleaning the window.
